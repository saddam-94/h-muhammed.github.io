Hossain Muhammad Saddam was born in Cumilla, Bangladesh in 1994. He received his B.Sc. (2018) from Computer Science and Engineering Department in The Shahjalal University of Science and Technology, Sylhet (SUST). In his time-span at SUST, he was learned programming language(C/C++, Java, Python), Data Structure, Algoirthm, Linear, Algebra, Calculas, Statistics, Probabiliy, OS, AI, and ML etc. He accomplished his final year thesis in 2017 at SUST supervised by Asst. Prof. Sabir Ismail and Asst. Prof. Md Saiful Islam. In his final year thesis, he worked on Natural Language Processing in Bengali word clustering using n-gram language model with probability distribution, and showed state of the art performance in this research area. Then he joined Chowagiken Corporation, Tokyo, Japan in 2019 as a machine learning engineer, and is working on Computer Vision and NLP as well as time series data.
